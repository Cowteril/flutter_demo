# Final Verdict

## Verdict

PASS.

## Recommendation

Use `FlutterWiz/flutter_video_feed` as the first integration spike.

## Reason

It is Flutter-based, MIT licensed, focused on vertical short-video feed behavior, and has a cleaner architecture than full TikTok/Firebase clones. That makes it friendlier for integrating our existing player overlays, emotion layer, TFLite gesture casting, and branch interaction UI.
